# QA-benchmark-polymers

QA dataset for benchmarking LLMs with respect to their abilities to answer general polymer science questions. 

### Dataset creation:
1. Generate QA (multiple choice) pairs with GPT-5.2 from pre-selected polymer reviews (56 reviews and prespectives about different areas of polymer science). 
2. Polymer expert review of all questions. Reshuffling of choice options.
